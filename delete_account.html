Account Deletion Information and Process

We're sorry to see you go! If you've decided to delete your Cineriot account, this page will guide you through the process and explain what happens to your data.

How to Delete Your Account

To delete your Cineriot account, please follow these steps:

  • Open the Cineriot app on your mobile device
  • Navigate to Settings from your profile menu
  • Tap on "Delete Account"
  • Follow the confirmation prompts to permanently delete your account

What Happens When You Delete Your Account

When you delete your Cineriot account, the following will occur:

  • Immediate Effect: Your account will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered
  • Profile Data: Your profile information, posts, comments, and likes will be removed
  • Social Content: All your shared cinema moments, reviews, and Fun Zone activities will be deleted
  • Rewards & Points: Any accumulated points, badges, and rewards will be forfeited
  • Subscriptions: Any active subscriptions or premium features will be cancelled
  • Messages: Private messages and chat history will be permanently deleted

Data Retention Policy

After account deletion:

  • Most personal data is deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Some anonymized analytics data may be retained for business purposes
  • Legal compliance may require certain data to be retained for specific periods
  • Backup systems may take additional time to fully remove all data traces

Before You Delete

Consider these alternatives before deleting your account:

  • Deactivate: Temporarily deactivate your account instead of permanent deletion
  • Privacy Settings: Adjust your privacy settings to limit data sharing
  • Content Backup: Download or save any important content you want to keep
  • Support: Contact our support team if you have concerns about your account

Need Help?

If you're experiencing issues with the app or have concerns about your account, our support team is here to help before you decide to delete your account.

Important Notice:

Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once deleted, you cannot recover your account, posts, followers, or any other data associated with your Cineriot profile.
