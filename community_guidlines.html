Community Guidelines

Building a Safe, Respectful & Professional Cinema Community

Welcome to Cineriot! We are a digital cinema platform designed for movie enthusiasts, professionals, and aspiring artists to connect, collaborate, and grow. To maintain a safe, respectful, and supportive space, we have established these Community Guidelines.

1. Professionalism & Authenticity

  • Be Yourself: Use your real identity if you are a professional (e.g., Director, Actor, Technician). Misrepresenting your credentials or impersonating someone else is prohibited.
  • Accurate Profiles: Provide truthful information in your profile, resume, portfolio, and job applications.
  • Constructive Communication: Maintain professionalism in messages, comments, and posts. Disagreements are okay, but personal attacks, insults, and offensive language are not tolerated.

2. Intellectual Property (Copyright & Personality Rights)

  • Respect Copyright: Do not post content (photos, videos, music, scripts) you do not own or have permission to use. This includes movie clips, trailers, songs, or copyrighted images without authorization.
  • Give Credit: If you share someone's work or idea, always credit the original creator.
  • No Deepfakes or Unauthorized AI-Generated Content: You are prohibited from uploading content that uses AI to replicate the likeness, voice, or identity of any individual without their explicit consent. This includes deepfakes, AI-generated impersonations, and similar misuse of technology.

3. Safety and Dignity

  • No Harassment or Bullying: Harassment, cyberbullying, stalking, doxxing (revealing private information), or any form of intimidation is strictly forbidden.
  • Zero Tolerance for Hate Speech: Discrimination or hate speech based on race, religion, caste, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or nationality is unacceptable.
  • No Abuse or Threats: Threatening violence, self-harm, or abuse (physical, emotional, or sexual) is prohibited.

4. Dangerous Content

  • No Illegal Activities: Do not post content promoting illegal activities such as drug use, piracy, hacking, or terrorism.
  • Self-Harm & Suicide: Content promoting self-harm, suicide, or eating disorders will be removed. If you or someone you know needs help, reach out to professional support services.

5. Nudity and Sexual Content

  • Artistic Context Only: Nudity or sexual content is only permitted within a clear artistic or educational cinema context (e.g., film scenes, behind-the-scenes footage). Graphic sexual content, pornography, or content exploiting minors is strictly prohibited.
  • Age-Appropriate: Our platform is for users 18+ only. Content must be appropriate for a professional cinema audience.

6. Spam and Platform Manipulation

  • No Spam: Do not repeatedly post the same content, send unsolicited messages, or engage in mass marketing.
  • Authentic Engagement: Do not use bots, fake accounts, or buy followers/likes to manipulate visibility or engagement.

7. Reporting and Enforcement

How to Report

If you see content or behavior that violates these guidelines, report it immediately using the in-app "Report" button or contact us at [Insert Email].

Consequences of Violations
  • Minor Violations: Warning and content removal.
  • Repeat Violations: Temporary account suspension.
  • Severe Violations: Permanent ban and, where necessary, reporting to law enforcement authorities.

8. Our Commitment

  • We strive to review reports quickly and fairly.
  • We are committed to creating a platform that reflects the diversity and creativity of the cinema industry.
  • We continuously update these guidelines to reflect community feedback and evolving legal requirements.

9. Legal Compliance

These guidelines are aligned with the Information Technology (IT) Act, 2000, the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, and other applicable Indian laws. By using Cineriot, you agree to comply with these guidelines and all relevant laws.
